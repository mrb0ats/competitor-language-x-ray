Use the format the user requests. If they do not choose, default to a chat brief.

- Proof/mechanism:

## Word Doc

Use a clean report:

1. Title and method
2. Executive summary
3. Competitor briefs
4. Offer and funnel comparison
5. Customer-language findings
6. Avatar and sub-avatar map
7. Angle, creative, PDP, and offer recommendations
8. Threat ranking

Keep tables readable and avoid huge quote dumps.

## Language Bank Rules

When reporting customer language:

- Use short exact phrases only when they carry important voice.
- Cluster phrases instead of listing dozens of raw comments.
- Preserve tone and context.
- Name the source type, such as Reddit, YouTube comment, TikTok comment, Amazon review, Trustpilot review, Etsy review, or PDP review.
- Avoid publishing sensitive personal details from comments.

## Threat Ranking Criteria

Rank ecommerce competitors by:

- offer strength: price architecture, bundles, subscription, shipping threshold, guarantee, warranty, trial, free gifts, urgency/scarcity
- funnel depth: ad path, PDP/landing page, advertorial, cart, checkout, upsells, retention/subscription, email/SMS signals
- tech-stack maturity: Shopify theme quality, visible apps, review stack, subscription/upsell/cart apps, email/SMS stack, loyalty/returns/support stack, and whether tools appear deliberately chosen or bloated
- paid-social maturity: visible ad volume, creative variety, UGC/native ads, refreshed hooks, landing-page variants
- proof strength: claims backed by credible evidence, UGC, reviews, demos, before/after, authority, comparison tables, trust assets
- momentum: ad volume, review growth/count, social following, traffic range, marketplace rank, creator/UGC activity
- customer-language fit: how precisely their copy matches repeated buyer desires, objections, and identity language
- vulnerability: repeated bad-review wounds, weak proof, weak offer, generic positioning, product defects, shipping/service complaints

Explain the ranking. Do not pretend it is a measured index unless you built a transparent scoring table.
